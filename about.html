About me

Eric Cross is a Canadian industrial designer with a hands-on approach to design that emphasizes quality and functionality. Based in Adelaide, AU, he has worked internationally for leading consultancies like Blue Sky Design Group in Australia and ITO Design in Switzerland.

In 2021, he founded the design consultancy Circ Industrial Design with long-time colleague and friend, Mugi Yamamoto. In addition to his client facing work, Eric also collaborates on experimental art and design projects to investigate new technologies and material applications.
