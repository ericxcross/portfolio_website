Qua Multipurpose Chair · ITOKI

Designer at ITO Design · 2018

Industrial design
Prototyping
Grasshopper 3D

Qua is a collaboration with ITOKI Japan to extend the Sequa product family with the addition of a lightweight multipurpose chair designed to suit new modes of work like hot-desking and collaboration. 

The flexible backrest uses variable wall thickness and an algorithmically generated hole pattern to create ‘living hinges’ that automatically adjust to the user's sitting position with limited adjustments required.
